Dining & Libations

The Urban Crust is hauling their mobile wood-fired kitchen to the venue! We will have a variety of luxurious pizzas (you can even make your own!), salads, apps, and more. There is also a full-bar in the reception area for you to enjoy!

What to wear?

Be comfortable! Ladies: spring or summer dress; Men: suits are not required.

Hey DJ!

We're taking song requests! Send us your suggestions and we'll pass them along to the DJ.

Transportation & Parking

There will be a valet at the restaurant, as well as several public parking lots attached or nearby: one attached to the restaurant, and two more across the street.
